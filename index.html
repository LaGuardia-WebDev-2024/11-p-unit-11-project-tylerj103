My Top 3 Animals That Are Adorable

  1. Animal #1

    Baby goats, or kids, are energetic, highly playful animals known for "stotting" (jumping/hopping) to develop coordination and agility. They require specialized care, including bottle-feeding or dam-raising, milk replacer until about 60 days old, and secure housing with draft-free, dry shelter..

  2. Animal #2

    The quokka is a small macropod about the size of a domestic cat. It is the only member of the genus Setonix. Like other marsupials in the macropod family, the quokka is herbivorous and mainly nocturnal. The quokka's range is a small area of southwestern Australia.

  3. Animal #3

    The red panda, also known as the lesser panda, is a small mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. It has dense reddish-brown fur with a black belly and legs, white-lined ears, a mostly white muzzle and a ringed tail.
